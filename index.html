T.E.A.M. 4 Travis

Allison Bones

BNI Goodyear Legacy

 

Does your Organization qualify for Arizona Tax Credits?

No. We do not qualify for Arizona tax credits, as our work is global, and not limited to helping only in AZ

What types of donations does your organization accept?

We accept – cash, sponsorships for our events, donations of prizes, gift cards and experiences for our annual TOPGOLF tournament, travel vouchers, in-kind donations

Organization's Mission

T.E.A.M. 4 Travis' mission is to prevent childhood death from the rare disease Isolated Congenital Asplenia (ICA). ICA occurs when children are born with no functioning spleen, compromising their immune systems and leaving them vulnerable to life-threatening bacterial infections. Undiagnosed ICA caused the death of 4-year-old Travis Bones. Our work is Travis’ legacy. We provide awareness and education about ICA, one of 10,000 known rare diseases, advocate for expanded Newborn Screening and Access to Genetic Counselors, and fund research on spleen development. We strive to change how rare diseases can be diagnosed quickly in newborns and children, ultimately saving lives.

BNI Future

 

Does your Organization qualify for Arizona Tax Credits?

Yes, we are QCO approved, which means we are a dollar-for-dollar charity. This means for 2023 taxes up until April 15, 2024 an individual can donate $421 and a married couple can donate $841 to our charity and receive that exact amount back. For 2024 taxes from now until April 2025 an individual can donate $470 and a married couple $938 and receive dollar for dollar back.

What types of donations does your organization accept?

We accept monetary donations. This donation is extremely important to us, as it is used to keep the charity open and the ability to keep serving the individuals in your community in need. It helps pay for housing for our individuals, court fees, move-in cost if needed to their new home, electricity, food, hygiene, and everything needed as they begin rebuilding their life. 100% of all donations goes to the service of our women. Our organization is run by volunteers only.

Due to the nature of our organization and the people we serve, we accept all types of donations.

We accept clothing, household items, kitchen items, outdoor furniture, vehicles, bikes, scooters, and appliances. We asked that all donations are in good working condition and ready to be placed in the home of someone in need. We also accept hygiene and cleaning supplies for donations as well.

Tears is more than just an organization. It is a family of people who share a bond that no one ever wishes to have – the loss of an infant.
